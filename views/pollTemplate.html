How does scoring work?

Every time you cast the vote, the thing/person you tapped on gets a point and the other one looses half a point. This means that the number shown beside each target is not the amount of votes.
This system heavily exaggerates the difference between things that are generaly favored and those that are not.
The number of total points in game is equal to the total amount of votes cast devided by two.
Scores can go negative. The results are shown without they're decimal places, but sould be sorted correctly.
